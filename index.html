Sitecore Pano Alpha

This is a demo of what is possible with a headless CMS that focuses on managing pure content. While many CMS solutions are used to build traditional websites (header, footer, page text and images), virtual experiences like this can also be powered by a CMS. Please select a sample panoramic scene below to explore what is possible. Images and hotspots are managed in Content Hub ONE as pure content. Right now only individual scenes can be explored. In the future multiple scenes will be linked together to create virtual tours (e.g. a university campus, the various hallways of a large hospital, a tourist destination such as a museum).
